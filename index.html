How It Began NEXTECH

In school, my guidance counselor called me to her office and told me there was an oppurtunity to join a program and learn computer science.

A huge thanks to Meghan Lewis for informing me about Nextech! Nextech is a Indianpolis based tech company who offered the Catapult program. The teaching of the class was instructed by teachers from Eleven Fifty. I learned intensively and couldn't thank them enough.

At first, I didn't think I was ready for the program , but after I joined, I realize that if I did not join this program, I wouldn't have the willpower that I do now. A big thanks to the people at Nextech and Eleven Fifty.

FROM A DREAM TO A REALITY

"When I was younger, I watched people and how people go about in life.

I realized that some people had been given oppurtunities and others had been thrown in the trash can of society. I was raised in a poor neighborhood. We could barely keep the clothes on our backs or the lights on, LITERALLY. I say this because you should never give up on your dreams and aspirations. I didn't think any of the oppurtunities would come to me, BUT they did. NEVER give up on your dreams, and once you believe in that dream, make it a reality." Nigel Green
